Jane Austen is a timeless writer. She wrote about humans, their qualities and flaws, with a great variety of characters and settings. She teaches valuable lessons about life and how circumstances can influence our choices without making them good or bad.

She is the kind of author that one can re-read all their life as one's perspective changes with age and experience.

She is a dear friend to read in times of need!
